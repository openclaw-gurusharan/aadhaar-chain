# Tokenization Opportunity: Credential Meets Asset

## Market Signals

### BlackRock Vision (Dec 2025)

Larry Fink (CEO) and Rob Goldstein (COO) on tokenization:

> "Tokenisation could greatly expand the world of investable assets beyond the listed stocks and bonds that dominate markets today."

**Key insights:**

- Tokenization is "next major evolution in market infrastructure"
- Two benefits: **instantaneous settlement** + **replacing paper with code**
- Real estate, infrastructure → smaller, accessible units
- **Digital identity verification** is a critical guardrail
- Early adoption in **developing world** where banking is limited

### Maharashtra Announcement (Oct 2025)

Devendra Fadnavis at Global Fintech Fest:

> "We will create a framework where you can tokenise your assets... Maharashtra will be India's first 'tokenised state'."

**Key announcements:**

- **₹50 trillion** dormant capital to unlock
- Focus on **real estate** - instant transactions, borrowings
- RBI launched **deposit tokenisation pilot**
- Target: **$1 trillion economy by 2030**
